  • designed numerous training courses beginning in the 1950s
  • married first Hedwiges "Vicky" Uzar and in 1960 they get divorced.

  • Joe Weider and Ben Weider were the co-founders of the International Federation of BodyBuilders
  • Joe Weider died on March 23, 2013, in Los Angeles, California, United States
